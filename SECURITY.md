## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you have detected a vulnerability in the terrarium-monitor code, you can report it to us by email at security[@]benoitparmentier.fr.

We will respond as soon as possible.

We thank you in advance for not disclosing the vulnerability publicly before we have had a chance to fix it.
